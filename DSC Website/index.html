About Us

The Day Scholars' Club of Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, Uttar Pradesh, established in 1984, is a place a Day Scholar truly belongs to, packed with mentorship, events, fun and laughter. It is a part of The Council of Student Activities. It has been the best site in Malaviyan campus for students to have fun, make life-long friendships and learn fundamental lessons of life. It builds strong connections, develop valuable skills, and makes you actively participate in events that enrich your college life and career opportunities.
